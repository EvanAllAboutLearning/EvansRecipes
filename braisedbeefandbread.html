Evan's Braised Beef & Bread

You, too, can make something as hearty and delicious as this!

Simply, and loosely, follow the list of ingredients below:

vi. Braised Beef (stew staples) Onions, Cheaper cut of beef, carrots, mushrooms, peppers, garlic, beef broth, cornmeal.

And here are the Directions:

  • smoke at 250 until internal hits 165
  • put in pan in the oven w/ beef broth & covered in foil at 300
  • 260g milk in microwave for 50 seconds

    • Instructions and also the materials for the hoagie buns:

    6g yeast; 3-5 mins 400g flour, 10g honey, 20g olive oil, 8g of salt rest 15 minutes, then knead
  • allow to rise for 1 hour
    • roll them out in cornmeal, rest for an hour and and bake steam with ice in cast iron
